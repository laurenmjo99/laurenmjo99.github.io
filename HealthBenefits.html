Health Benefits of Coffee

Coffee is more than just your morning pick-me-up. In fact, coffee has been shown to have some significant health benefits!

What are the Health Benefits of a Daily Cup O' Joe?

Recent studies have revealed correlations between regularly drinking coffee and a reduced risk of multiple diseases. Although there are many more benefits, here are 5 ways drinking coffee can significantly improve your health.

  1. Coffee contains caffeine - a psychoactive stimulant. Consuming caffeine enhances the firing neurons, and improving overall brain function and reaction time.
  2. Another benefit of coffee's high caffeine level is that it can also aid in the body's natural fat burning process.
  3. Caffeine can also stimulate the nervous system and trigger the release of the "fight-or-flight" hormone, epinephrine. This significantly improves physical or athletic performances.
  4. Coffee contains many important daily nutrients like vitamins B2, B5, B3, manganese, and potassium.
  5. In recent studies, regularly drinking coffee has been correlated to a lower risk of developing Dementia, Alzheimers, Parkinson's, and Type II Diabetes.

Coffee is so much more than just the perfect drink to wake you up each morning. Drinking coffee can increase your vitamin levels, alertness, and physical performance while also decreasing risk of some diseases - so drink up!
