The Daily Grind

With a Simple Fresh Brew, There’s Nothing You Can’t Do!

With its rich flavor and history, coffee has become a staple in the lives of people across the world. Originally discovered in Ethiopia, coffee has made its way into the kitchens and coffee houses of billions of people.

Coffee comes in many forms. From espresso to lattes to affogatos, there is bound to be a coffee drink for everyone to enjoy. Not to mention all the different roasts that are available in beans or ground. If you think you don't like coffee - you just haven't tried the right kind yet!

In addition to its complex flavor, drinking coffee has many health benefits. From getting essential vitamins to lowering disease risk, there is so much to get from a cup of Joe every morning.
